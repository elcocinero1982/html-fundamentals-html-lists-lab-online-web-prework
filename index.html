• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices os cheese
  • cheddar
  • mozzarella
  • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Step to make a grilled cheese

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bnread in frying pan and fry
  3. add cheese on top of bread
  4. cover with second slice of bread
  5. turn over and fry for two minutes
